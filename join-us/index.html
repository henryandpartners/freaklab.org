Think Beyond. Make Futures.

FREAK Lab is an anti-disciplinary research cluster that converges arts, science, and technology for thinking beyond and exploring the symbiotic relationship between human and emerging technologies.

Founded by JSTP alumni and mentors in 2017 at KMUTT, we use various methods and mediums — prototypes, research, arts, and installations — to transform futuristic ideas into practical prototypes.

"When we come together, our potential is limitless!"

From Space to BioDesign

Our projects span from space exploration (rocket tests, Blue Origin payloads, lunar pearls) to biodesign (wearable biofab, environmental sensors) and everything in between.
