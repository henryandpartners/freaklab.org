Research for the Futures

From Biodigital to Space Research! FREAK Lab conducts cutting-edge research across seven research groups, each exploring the intersection of arts, science, and technology.

🧬

Biodigital

Bio-digital interfaces, wearable biotechnology, living systems, and the convergence of biological and digital worlds. Projects include iSnail environmental sensors, BioFab wearable devices, and HackBiodesign.

🚀

C.A.T Space (Cat Space)

Space exploration, rocketry, microgravity research, and astrobiotechnology. Thailand's first space missions, solid-propellant rocket tests, passive payloads on Blue Origin, and the Pearl of Lunar project.

🍽

Fab Taste

3D food printing, food fabrication, holographic food, and the futures of gastronomy. Exploring neo-print capitalism and food as a medium for future-making.

⚡

Functional + Aesthetics

Where function meets beauty. Projects that bridge design thinking with engineering, from social robot design to STEAM education.

🧠

Neuro Media

Neuroscience, EEG, brain-computer interfaces, and the merger of biological and machine intelligence. Research on visual short-term memory, attentional modulation, and functional MRI.

📚

Learning Futures

Future education, STEM learning, creative pedagogy, and digital constructivism. Projects include Tele-Creativity, the JSTP Planet project, and AR for history education.

🌍

Experimental Society

Social experiments, alternative currencies (Globeyond), speculative futures, and societal transformation through design and technology.
